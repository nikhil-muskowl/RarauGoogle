Why Birthday?


Who can use RaRaU

When people stand behind their opinions and actions, our community is safer and more accountable. For this reason, you must: Use the same name that you use in everyday life. Provide accurate information about yourself. Create only one account (your own) and use your timeline for personal purposes. Not share your password, give access to your RaRaU account to others or transfer your account to anyone else (without our permission).
